Embed Your Sketchfab Models Straight Into Your Website

To put your photogrammetry model onto your website, first publish your model onto SketchFab. This is a platform that allows users to freely upload and download 3D models. It is designed as a way to share information and gain free access to others' public models.

You can create a basic account on SketchFab for free, which allows you to publish your own models that yopu upload, and to stylize in a number of ways, such as adding notiations. To upload higher quality or larger models (in terms of data), as well as access other resources and more bells and whitsles, you can upgrade your account to different packages with a monthly subscription.

Embedding a model into your website is incredibly easy to do. After publishing your model, you can then click on the "embed" option which brings up a small menu which lets you customize how users of your website will interact with the model, and gives you the code for your model wrapped in a div containter that all you have to do is simply copy and paste into your index.html code (or on whichver page you would like).
